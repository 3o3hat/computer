The oddly named West London group will meet at: The Mad Hatter, 3-7 Stamford Street, London, SE1 9NY, on Wednesday 15th January, from about 18:30.


Come to the pub, have a drink, and say "Computer". Meet other people who use computers, and talk about interesting things. (You could also learn to cross-stitch a space invader, or read some copies of Amiga Power. Also, rather close to the pub is a building that is upside down! Look out for that.)

    The oddly named West London group met at: The Mad Hatter, 3-7 Stamford Street, London, SE1 9NY, on Thursday 24th October 2013, from about 18:30.

    Copies of Amiga Power were read, space invaders and Pac-Man ghosts were cross-stitched, people attempted regular expression crosswords, and read print-outs of esoteric languages such as the My Little Pony inspired FiM++. Cider, lemonade and lime, beer, and other such beverages were drunk, pies were eaten. Stickers were stuck. (Thanks Tef!) And also, most importantly, many wonderful conversations were had.
