Computer Anonymous - Boston

Read the overview if you haven't already.

We meet on the last Wednesday of each month at 7pm, generally at pubs in the Camberville area.

If you're new: usually, there will be some anons hanging out in front of the venue until 7:10 or so, waiting for others to show up, so look for them and say hi! You can also try tweeting some of the folks listed under "the people" -- @patcable makes it to most meetups. We haven't had a @WhyCompBos meetup in a while, and @kmbrlynn is trying to revive it! So you can reach out to her too.
