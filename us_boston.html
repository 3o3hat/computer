Computer Anonymous - Boston

Read the overview if you haven't already.

We meet on the last Wednesday of each month at 7pm, generally at pubs in the Cambridge-ish area.

If you're new: generally, there will be some anons hanging out in front of the venue until 7:10 or so, waiting for others to show up, so look for them and say hi! You can also try tweeting some of the folks listed under "the people"--@flowerhack makes it to most meetups and has recently set up Twitter on her dumbphone
